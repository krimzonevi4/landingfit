Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your FitLife journey.

What if I'm completely new to HIIT or fitness in general?

Perfect! Our HIIT Fundamentals program is specifically designed for beginners. Every trainer is certified in proper form instruction, and we'll modify every exercise to match your current fitness level. You'll start slow and build up gradually.

How is 30 minutes really enough for an effective workout?

HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) has been scientifically proven to burn more calories and improve cardiovascular health more effectively than traditional cardio. Our 30-minute sessions include warm-up, main workout, and cool-down, maximizing every minute.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule classes?

No problem! You can cancel or reschedule any class up to 2 hours before start time through our mobile app. We understand busy schedules change, so we've made it as flexible as possible.

Do you offer any guarantees on results?

While individual results vary, we're so confident in our program that we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you attend at least 12 classes in your first month and aren't satisfied, we'll refund your membership fee.

What should I bring to my first class?

Just bring yourself, a water bottle, and comfortable workout clothes. We provide all equipment, towels, and have changing rooms with lockers. Heart rate monitors are available for loan or purchase.
